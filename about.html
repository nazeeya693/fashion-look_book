About the Lookbook

The Fashion Lookbook is a pastel-themed project created to showcase modern aesthetic fashion trends. Designed with soft baby pink colors, this lookbook highlights outfits, styles, and color palettes that inspire creativity and confidence.

This website is part of a mini-project created by Nazee, combining clean UI, pastel colors, and subtle animations to give users a magazine-style browsing experience.
